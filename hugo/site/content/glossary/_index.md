A plain-English reference for the financial terms used across this site. Each entry explains what the metric means, how it's calculated, and how to use it when evaluating stocks.

## Terms

| Term | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| [Beta](/glossary/beta/) | Volatility relative to the market |
| [Dividend Yield](/glossary/dividend-yield/) | Annual dividend as a percentage of price |
| [EPS (Earnings Per Share)](/glossary/eps/) | Company profit per outstanding share |
| [Forward P/E Ratio](/glossary/forward-pe/) | Price relative to expected future earnings |
| [Market Capitalization](/glossary/market-cap/) | Total market value of a company |
| [Moving Average (50-Day)](/glossary/moving-average-50/) | Average closing price over 50 trading days |
| [Moving Average (200-Day)](/glossary/moving-average-200/) | Average closing price over 200 trading days |
| [P/E Ratio](/glossary/pe-ratio/) | Price relative to current earnings |
| [Price-to-Book Ratio](/glossary/price-to-book/) | Price relative to company's book value |
| [RSI (Relative Strength Index)](/glossary/rsi/) | Momentum indicator: overbought vs oversold |
| [52-Week High](/glossary/52-week-high/) | Highest price in the past year |
| [52-Week Low](/glossary/52-week-low/) | Lowest price in the past year |
| [Volume](/glossary/volume/) | Number of shares traded |
