How does it work?

The extractor uses a non-greedy backtracking algorithm to find all valid ticker symbols within any string of text — handling overlapping matches, common English words that look like tickers (like "IT", "A", or "ARE"), and multi-word company names. Once symbols are found, they're matched against the live database of {{ len .Site.Data.all_tickers.tickers }} tickers and scored across five investment strategy lenses.
