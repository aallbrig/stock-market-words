About NVIDIA

NVIDIA Corporation designs and sells graphics, compute, and networking solutions worldwide. Its Data Center segment — the source of nearly all FY2025 revenue growth — serves AI training and inference workloads via H100/H200 GPUs and the NVLink interconnect fabric. Gaming, Professional Visualization, and Automotive make up the remainder. Founded 1993 · Santa Clara, CA · 42,000 employees.
