About NVIDIA

NVIDIA Corporation provides graphics and compute and networking solutions in the United States, Taiwan, China, and internationally. The company's Graphics segment offers GeForce GPUs for gaming and PCs, Quadro/NVIDIA RTX GPUs for enterprise workstations, and cloud GPU instances. Its Compute & Networking segment offers Data Center platforms including GPUs, CPUs, DPUs, networking, and interconnects for AI, data analytics, and HPC. NVIDIA is headquartered in Santa Clara, California, with 42,000 employees. Founded 1993 · United States · Semiconductors.
