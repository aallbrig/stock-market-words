Why does this exist?

Stock ticker symbols hide everywhere in financial writing — in news articles, analyst reports, earnings call transcripts, even social media. Reading "Nvidia" doesn't tell you the ticker is NVDA. "Ford" could be F. A Reddit post about "the cat sat on the mat" accidentally contains CAT and MAT.

This tool solves that. Paste any text, see every valid ticker the text contains, and immediately get strategy scores for each one — so you can decide what to look into further.
